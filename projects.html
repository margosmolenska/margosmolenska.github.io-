PROJECTS
Choreography
Dance is about self-expression, control over body, and creativity. In series of videos for dancer and choreographer Kristina Gorbach, I tried to translate all of these components of dance. My work includes both filming the choreography and editing the video.
Product video
When doing a product a video, I concentrate on a product rather than on editing skills. Our obstacle was to show the new Yearbook of 2020 and communicate its' simple and trendy design. Even though there are several references to pandemic, we also transmited the sense of community. Of course, the amount of Yearbooks is limited so you better hurry up to buy it!
Social projects
Dressember is a social movement designed to bring awareness about sex human trafficking. Unfortunately, our world is a dangerous place; therefore, such organisations arise to help victims all around the globe. I also took part in spreading information about this drastic issue by producing, filming, and editing a Dressember campaign organized by LCC International University students.
Fashion video
Do you want a personal fashion video? Well, you contacted the right person! Fashion film is one of my favorite genre: I enjoy showing the personality and uniqueness of my client. In this work, features a model Sofia Azarova which inspired me for the video with her gorgeous looks.
